OUR WHY

Boys deserve better information.

We create accurate, age-appropriate, and emotionally intelligent resources that help boys understand their bodies, their feelings, and what it truly means to grow into healthy men.

The Puberty Box
Asynchronous • Self-Paced • Ages 10–15

Three carefully designed tracks that meet boys at their exact stage of development. Every lesson is medically accurate, emotionally supportive, and created with input from boys themselves.

Video lessons (8–18 per track)
Printable workbooks & journals
Private parent + boy community
FOUNDATIONS OF CHANGE

Gentle introduction as puberty begins

Boys at this age are just starting to notice changes. Our focus is on reducing anxiety, building vocabulary, and creating a safe foundation of knowledge and self-acceptance.

Key Topics Covered:
  • Reproductive anatomy (external parts — proper names & functions)
  • Reproduction defined (simple, age-appropriate explanation of how babies are made)
  • Normal physical changes (growth spurts, skin, body odor)
  • Emotional awareness & naming feelings
  • Hygiene basics & self-care routines
Program Details
Duration: 4–5 weeks (self-paced)
Videos: 9 short lessons (4–7 min each)
Includes: Illustrated workbook + parent discussion guide
Outcome: Boys can confidently name body parts and understand that changes are normal and healthy
NAVIGATING THE SURGE

Peak changes + deeper understanding

This is often the most intense and confusing period. We go deeper into anatomy, introduce consent and respect, and help boys build healthy habits and emotional regulation skills.

Key Topics Covered:
  • Full reproductive anatomy (internal + external)
  • Reproduction defined (fertilization, basic genetics, pregnancy)
  • Voice changes, facial hair, muscle growth, wet dreams
  • Consent, boundaries, and respecting others
  • Body image, peer pressure & mental health basics
Program Details
Duration: 6 weeks (self-paced)
Videos: 14 lessons (5–9 min each)
Includes: Personal journal + “Talk to Your Parent” cards
Outcome: Boys understand consent, can explain reproduction accurately, and feel equipped to handle rapid changes
OWNING YOUR FUTURE

Preparation for young adulthood

Older boys are ready for more advanced topics and real-world application. We emphasize personal responsibility, healthy relationships, and long-term health decisions.

Key Topics Covered:
  • Advanced reproductive anatomy & fertility
  • Reproduction defined (full process + contraception overview)
  • Hormones, self-exams, when to see a doctor
  • Healthy vs. unhealthy relationships & communication
  • Positive masculinity, purpose, and emotional leadership
Program Details
Duration: 7–8 weeks (self-paced)
Videos: 18 lessons (6–11 min each)
Includes: “Future Self” vision workbook + STI & consent deep dive
Outcome: Boys leave with a clear understanding of their bodies, values, and how to make informed choices
